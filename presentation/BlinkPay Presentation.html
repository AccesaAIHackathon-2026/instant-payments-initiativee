BlinkPay — SEPA Instant Payments & Digital Euro
Team: Mihai Serban  ·  Petru Scurtu  ·  Marius Cocis
Solution Overview SEPA Instant Payments for EU Tier-2/3 Banks
Problem
EU Reg. 2024/886 mandates SEPA Instant for all PSPs by Jan 2025, yet most tier-2/3 banks lack the infrastructure. Building from scratch is expensive, complex, and slow.
Solution
BlinkPay is an end-to-end instant payment platform: mobile wallet (QR, NFC, biometric SCA), bank PSP (proxy lookup, VoP, Digital Euro custody), FIPS clearing, and merchant POS. Built as a modular POC in one day with real-time settlement and live flow visualization.
Key Impact
Full SCT Inst flow in 1 day vs months. Reusable architecture cuts integration effort ~60%. Sub-2s settlement with real-time merchant notification.
Technology Stack AI-Assisted Full-Stack
  • AI: Claude Code (Opus 4.6) — plan, code, debug, commit
  • Frontend: React 19 + TypeScript + Vite
  • Mobile: Android Kotlin, Jetpack Compose, CameraX
  • Backend: Java 21 + Spring Boot 3.x, ISO 20022
  • Infra: Docker Compose, nginx, SSE events
AI Insights: Limitations Infrastructure & tooling challenges
  • Network restrictions: Accesa corporate network blocked phone-laptop connectivity, requiring extensive workarounds
  • Binary files: Claude cannot read/edit PPTX, images, or binary formats directly
  • Context pressure: large codebases need careful context management across long sessions
AI Insights: Best Practices What worked well
  • CLAUDE.md upfront: domain context and API contracts saved hours of back-and-forth
  • Plan mode first: design before coding produced coherent multi-component systems
  • Parallel agents: multiple search agents simultaneously cut research time dramatically
  • Iterative commits: small, frequent commits kept the codebase stable throughout

Competitive Advantage

Why SEPA Instant beats card networks — and where BlinkPay fits vs Wero
$111B
Visa and Mastercard collected $111 billion in interchange fees globally in 2024.
Merchants pay 1–2% on every card swipe — passed on to consumers as higher prices. In the US, swipe fees are retailers' largest cost after labor. Even in the EU (capped at 0.2–0.3%), processing costs reach billions.
SEPA Instant eliminates the middleman. BlinkPay brings merchant fees to zero.
vs Visa / Mastercard Account-to-Account beats Card Networks
  • Zero merchant fees — no interchange, no acquirer, no scheme fees (vs 1-2% on cards)
  • Instant settlement — <10 seconds vs 1-3 business days with cards
  • No chargebacks — irrevocable once settled (vs 120-day dispute window)
  • No hardware — no POS terminals or EMV certification. Just QR or NFC
  • EU sovereign rails — no US network dependency
  • Regulatory mandate — EU Reg. 2024/886 makes SCT Inst mandatory
vs Wero (EPI) — Our Positioning Same rails, different market
Same SCT Inst backbone, different market:
  • Wero = closed club — 16 major EU banks. Hundreds of tier-2/3 banks excluded
  • BlinkPay = open platform — any bank can adopt, no consortium needed
  • Digital Euro native — built-in from day one (Wero: "future channel")
  • Merchant-first — QR + NFC POS, no Wero onboarding needed
