BlinkPay

SEPA Instant Payments & Digital Euro
Team: Mihai Serban  ·  Petru Scurtu  ·  Marius Cocis

Problem

EU Regulation 2024/886 mandates full SEPA Instant capability for all eurozone PSPs by October 2025, with non-eurozone EU banks following by 2027. Most tier-2/3 banks still lack the infrastructure. Building from scratch is expensive, complex, and slow.

Solution

BlinkPay is an end-to-end instant payment platform: mobile wallet (QR, NFC, biometric SCA), bank PSP (proxy lookup, VoP, Digital Euro custody), FIPS clearing, and merchant POS. Built as a modular POC in one day with real-time settlement and live flow visualization.

Key Impact

Full SCT Inst flow in 1 day vs months. Reusable architecture cuts integration effort ~60%. Sub-2s settlement with real-time merchant notification.

Competitive Advantage

$111B
Visa and Mastercard collected $111 billion in interchange fees globally in 2024. Merchants pay 1-2% on every card swipe - passed on to consumers as higher prices. SEPA Instant eliminates the middleman. BlinkPay brings merchant fees to zero.
vs Visa / Mastercard
  • Zero merchant fees - no interchange, no acquirer, no scheme fees
  • Instant settlement - <10 seconds vs 1-3 business days
  • No chargebacks - irrevocable once settled
  • No hardware - no POS terminals or EMV certification
  • EU sovereign rails - no US network dependency
  • Regulatory mandate - EU Reg. 2024/886 makes SCT Inst mandatory
vs Wero (EPI)
  • Wero = closed club - 16 major EU banks only
  • BlinkPay = open platform - any bank, no consortium
  • Digital Euro native - built-in from day one
  • Merchant-first - QR + NFC POS, no onboarding
